About Me

My name is Alex Lopez. I am 26 years old and have decided to finally (and seriously) take charge of my life aspirations.

I studied philosophy in college and for the entire time I thought I would end up sitting in an office reading through endless documents as an attorney.

About a year and a half ago I had enough of trying to become something I was only doing to satisfy others, and I decided to pursue what I really like to do, which is creating websites!

When I'm not coding, I train in both Muay Thai and Jui-Jitsu. I love to go for runs and be outdoors. When I'm home, I enjoy coding random programs and listening to good music.

My mom has a saying in spanish, "querer es poder." It could be translated in different ways but I like to translate it as "wanting is power." If I want something then I have the power to go after it and that's exactly what has kept me going on this journey. I'm excited to continue learning and developing my skills as a programmer.
