The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Other Guys

A great movie about two cops who've had enough being the other guys.

Friendzone

A really funny French comedy about a guy who decides to stop being a push-over to finally get a girlfriend.

No Manches Frida

A Mexican Rom-Com about a substitute teacher, who is actually an ex-convict, that just wants to get his money back, but he falls in love with one of the teachers and decides to change his ways.
